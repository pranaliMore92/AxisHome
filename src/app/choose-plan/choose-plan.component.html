Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1) What is ICICI Bharat Griha Raksha?

A Bharat Griha Raksha policy offers protection to the structure and contents of the house in the event of any physical destruction or damage incurred to the house by any unavoidable perils. ICICI Bharat Griha Raksha provides coverage to a house and its contents against loss or damage caused by natural calamities (flood, storm, landslide, etc.)

2) What losses does the policy cover?

ICICI Bharat Griha Raksha covers losses/damage to content due to natural and man-made calamities mentioned below.

  • Fire
  • Lightening
  • Explosion/Implosion
  • Aircraft Damage
  • Impact Damage
  • Riot, Strike, Malicious Damage
  • Storm, Cyclone, Typhoon, Tempest, Hurricane, Tornado, Flood and Inundation
  • Subsidence and Landslide including Rockslide
  • Bursting and/or overflowing of water tanks, apparatus and pipes
  • Missile Testing Operations
  • Leakage from Automatic Sprinkler Installation
  • Bush Fire
  • Volcano
  • Earthquake
  • Terrorism
3) What Does Auto escalation of 10% of sum insured every year mean?

Escalation of 10% will be applicable on base sum insured every Year

4) Whether Auto Escalation is valid for Annual Policy?

Yes, Auto escalation is valid for Annual Policy and is calculated on pro-rata basis

5) What am I to do in case of a claim?

You may register your claim by calling 24 hrs call centre 1800-2666. Within <TAT> working days of your claim registration, our claims officer will contact you to guide you on the damage survey and claim procedure thereafter.​

6) What are the documents required for filing a claim?

The following are the basic documents required for filing a claim:

  • Duly filled and completed Claim form
  • Policy Copy
  • Final bills of repairers
  • Fire Brigade report, FIR, Investigation report of police, if required
  • Any other documents requested by surveyor or claims manager

Our claims officer will ensure that the claim form is sent to you once you register your claim with our call centre.

7) Why is a list of damaged items not required?

Insurance works on good faith. We as an insurance company trust our customers. Therefore, the specific and detailed listing of your contents is not always necessary when you buy ICICI Bharat Griha Raksha from us. However, preserving the bills helps in faster claims settlement. In the absence of bills, alternate evidence will be taken into consideration.

8) Whether all the Individuals in the family can be covered under Personal Accident cover? Is there any sum insured capping?

Personal Accident Coverage is limited to Insured and its spouse. In case of death of Insured & / its spouse (after occurrence of Insured) - 5 Lakhs per person can be claimed from the insurance company.

9) Will partial damage due to fire inside the flat be covered?​

Yes partial and total loss both shall be covered due to an insured peril

10) Will the policy cover defects in a building due to electric short circuits?​

Any loss or damage due to fire due to short circuits is covered, any electrical breakdown due to short circuit where no fire is observed is excluded from scope of cover.

11) If I do not possess purchase receipts of my house contents, how will you determine its age (to calculate depreciation) and value? While the question of age arises specifically in terms of electronics and furniture, the question of value arises in terms of furniture, electronics and jewelry too.​

We shall appreciate the list of all items with pictures which is optional to support your claim.

1) What types of dwelling will be covered under Complete Home Protect Policy?

Fully built and operational Flats, Individual apartments, Bunglows, Row Houses.

2) Do we cover structure for Market value under Home Insurance?

No, Structure will be covered on the basis of re-instatment value only and customer will get the claim for re-instating or replacing the property in case the property gets damaged/destroyed due to specified perils.

3) What is the tenure for Structure and Contents?

In this product, the customer can opt for a tenure upto 5 years.

4) What is not covered under Structure?

Kutcha Construction is not covered under the policy. Property other than RCC Construction is also not covered under the policy.

5) What is not covered under Contents?

Portable electronics equipment's such as mobile, laptops are not covered under the policy. Contents older than 10 years shall be excluded from scope of cover, contents kept outside home premises, Manufacturing defects, Cash, Pedal cycle & automobile, Plate glass(until mentioned specifically) are into covered.

6) What about seepage and leakage in walls when there is heavy rains?

Rainfall is not an insured peril and any damages due to rainfall will not be covered under the policy.

7) Is there any age limit on Home Building covered under this product?

Yes, we do not cover buildings more tha 50 years old until specified.

8) In short circuit covered under the policy?

In case of loss or damage to structure/content of the insured premises due to fire arising from a short curcuit is covered. However, breakdown of equipment due to short circuit is not covered until the customer has opted for coverage against section 3(Mechanical/Electrical breakdown).

9) What is covered under valuables?​

Items such as jewellery, silverware, paintings, works of art, antique items, curios and items of similar nature.

10) Is any​ section mandatory in this product for policy issuance?

Section 1 and any one other section is mandatory for policy issuance.
